This curiosity was born while attending an online workshop, "Creative and Artistic Use of Medical Imagery" led by Haraldur Karlsson and organised by Va≈°ulka Kitchen Brno in April-May 2020.

Since the workshop, I have been testing material in terms of footage (filmed, found, images in motion) and colours. My aim for the future is to convert these objects into 3D files that can be used in 3D and VR environments.
