We have adapted [Insider](/projects/live/insider) into installations for opening events to reach audiences outside the performance schedules. Each installation explores various elements of the work while adapting to the theme of the hosting event.

These installations were created by a collective of artists - Cristina Maldonado, Keya Singh, Eva Rosemarijn, Lea Kukovičič, Isa Juchniewicz, Lamija Čehajić. Our individual participation at each event changes as needed.
