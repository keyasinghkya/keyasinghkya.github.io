keya singh is a prague-based visual artist working with video, installation, and performance. currently studying at the academy of fine arts prague, her practice navigates audiovisual manipulation, blending cultural and personal elements to create hybrid narratives that feel simultaneously intimate and borrowed. she's interested in how identity and popular culture shape our sense of reality, using humor and emotional resonance to question these constructions. her work has been exhibited and performed across europe, india, and mexico in both solo and collaborative contexts.
she often wishes she were a rock.
